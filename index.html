My Story

I'm an extrovert who loves collaborative problem solving. In college, I trained React Native developers and ran a product development consultancy where I built MVPs with full-stack JavaScript tools.

At SpaceX I was the youngest software engineer put on the Enterprise Information Systems Build Reliability team, whose mission-critical software caught product defects and tracked rocket assembly issues.

After SpaceX, I decided to take a stab at running my own business and building my own product. That taught me the importance of sales alongside engineering and design. Most recently, I've been co-founder of IvyBridge, a B2B education expert network and teaching services marketplace. I closed $12k/mo deals and built the expert catalog product in Gatsby, Redux, Node.js, and Airtable.

How I Think About Product

As a UI engineer and designer, I love thinking about human-computer interaction. The best products are delightful, and almost seem to read their users' minds.

I love working cross-functionally to build out magical user experiences that achieve business goals and heighten user engagement.
